My Work

Graphics and Special Effects
Recently, I have been developing and experimenting with my graphic design and special effects skills, using my own skills and online tutorials I have been testing what I can do and create some cool demonstrations.
Realistic Earth Simulation
December 2017

I wanted to explore the ways worlds can be created from scratch, so I decided to make a short simulation of the Earth from space. This involved incorporating a lot of layers and also lighting effects to make the graphic realistic.
Petition Graphic
November 2017

To provide a cut away within my documentary about British Sign Langauge, I created a short animation which displays the statistics on a current petition. This was created by screen shotting the webpage of the current petition and replicating elements through shapes which can then be animated, helping the image come to life.
Map Travel Animation
September 2017

To add some addition visual to my Zambia project I decided to make an animation that traces the route from England to Zambia. This is done by taking an image and applying the route path which is then revealed by changing the mask path throughout the duration. Also, by making the camera 3D, it gives a new perspective of view.
