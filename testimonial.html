(July-October 2017)

Matthew interpreted the brief well and considering the scholarship had not taken place, it was unclear what media would be available until the elective have been completed. Aegon Classic Birmingham - Events Volunteer and Media Team Assistant
June 2017
"The Aegon Classic Birmingham 2017 is a WTA Premier women's grass court tennis tournament hosted at The Edgebaston Priory Club and organised by the Lawn Tennis Association. The event featured a number of the world's top players - including two-time Wimbledon champion Petra Kvitova, former French Open champion Gabine Muguruza and current British no.1 Johanna Konta - and saw 2000 spectators attend on a daily basis.

Matt was a brilliant member of the 100-strong team of volunteers. He volunteered for five consecutive days and his roles uncluded being a Centre Court Steward and a Match Court Steward, helping check tickets, manage crowds and ensure the event was running smoothly.

Matt did tell me he was studying a media course and requested if he could gain some time and experience with the media teams present at the tournament. So for some of his time with us, Matt was able to shadow photographers, journalists and the tv crews as well as getting involved with what happens in the media centre.

Matt was totally reliable, incredible enthusiastic and relentlessly committed; it was a real joy having him as part of the team. Despite the extremely hot weather and very long days - testing conditions for any volunteer - Matt always carried out his duties to the highest possible standard. He also did an excellent job working alongside fellow volunteers and professionals, integrating seamlessly into a large, diverse team and, ultimately, the event as a whole!"

Fantastic work Matt - please come back next year!
Hugh Stirling, Volunteer Manager
